About Snapshots
  • Snapshots capture the current consensus values of all KPIs at a specific point in time
  • Use snapshots to track progress over time (weekly, monthly, quarterly)
  • Compare snapshots to see trends: ↗️ improving, ↘️ declining, → stable
  • Recommended: Create snapshots at regular intervals (e.g., end of each sprint, month, or quarter)
  • Snapshot labels help organize: "Q1 2026", "Sprint 5", "Baseline", "Go-Live"
