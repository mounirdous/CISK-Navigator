Supporting Dimensions
The Foundations of CISK
🧭
8W (Bachman+)
The 8W model extends the classic 5W framework — Why, Who, How, What, Where, When, HowMuch, Whereas — to define a complete governance rhythm through Power Circles and structured routines.
🧠
Cognitive
CISK is built around human cognition — sense-making under ambiguity, distributed decision-making, and the maieutic facilitation of collective intelligence across organizational layers.
🎯
Mission
Every CISK entity — from a Challenge down to a KPI — is anchored to a mission statement that answers why it exists, ensuring strategic coherence across all operational levels.
📐
Dimensions
The framework operates across four strategic dimensions: Strategic, Tactic, Operational, Historic — each mapped to a CISK operator and governed by its own cadence and toolset.
🌍
Anthropology
Organizations are treated as social systems — tressées (braided) quasi-decomposable structures where culture, trust, and shared rituals are as critical as processes and technology.
