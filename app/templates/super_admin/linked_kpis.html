About Linked KPI Values

Live Read-Through: Consumer KPIs automatically read values from their source KPIs in real-time. No data is copied or imported - values are always current.

Deletion Protection: Source KPIs cannot be deleted while they have active consumers. The link must be removed first.

Independent Targets: Consumer KPIs can set their own targets even though they read values from a source.
