Common SMTP Providers

SendGrid
Host: smtp.sendgrid.net Port: 587 (TLS) Username: apikey Password: Your API key
Mailgun
Host: smtp.mailgun.org Port: 587 (TLS) Username: Your Mailgun SMTP username
Brevo (Sendinblue)
Host: smtp-relay.brevo.com Port: 587 (TLS) Free: 300 emails/day
Configuration Tips
  • Most providers use port 587 with TLS
  • Gmail requires an App Password (not your regular password)
  • SendGrid and Mailgun offer free tiers
  • Always test the configuration before enabling notifications
  • Check your SMTP provider's rate limits
  • Use a no-reply email address as sender
